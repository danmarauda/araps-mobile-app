Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign in?
Use your ARA Property Services email via WorkOS AuthKit, Sign in with Apple, or tap "View Demo" to explore with sample data.

How do I enable Face ID / Touch ID?
Sign in once, then go to Settings ‚Üí Security and enable biometric authentication.

How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data.

How do I clear my chat history?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Data ‚Üí Clear Chat History.

The QR scanner isn't working
Make sure you've granted camera access. Go to iPhone Settings ‚Üí ARAPS Mobile ‚Üí Camera and enable access.

I can't log in with WorkOS
Ensure your account is active with ARA Property Services. Contact your IT administrator if access has been revoked.
